What is HOPE?

A circular economy project based on recycling our everyday wastes to produce dry food, wet food and animal feed by providing job opportunities to the poor families and teach them how to work on that project and marketing them among the shelters and pets shops.

We decided that the people who will work on this project are the poor families, we will teach them the process at the factory production lines and we will market the product for them among the shelters and the pet shops and all the fund will go for them, and as a result of that they will produce our product having lower price than the market by 60% we will contribute in helping the shelters and the animal savers to save more stray dogs and increase animal savings and protection and all of that by the corporation of our success partners which are all of the shelters, factories, restaurants, hotels and animal saver and their names will be recorded in the first environmental map in Egypt the CSR which is the corporate social responsibilities and by that we'll be standing side by side to all society segments to save more innocent lives and that's why we call it HOPE!

Our vision

Creating a better world having more sympathy for stray dogs and homeless animals to save more pure lives.

Approved

Creative

Our project based on hotels, restaurants and factories wastes instead of burning or throwing it we recycled them to produce dry food, wet food and animal feed.

Supporting
